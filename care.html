It is very important to care for your very own pet marshmallow, with the correct care and love your pet marshmallow can live as long as a Twinkie.

  1. Love Your Marshmallow

    Your pet marshmallow needs love and attention. Not alot is required just a small pat on the head or words of praise everyday shoudl kepp it happy.

  2. Don't Toast

    Do not expose your marshmallow to open flame. Despite popular belief, marshmallows generally do not enjoy being "toasted".

  3. Don't Get Wet

    Do not get your marshmallow wet. Yes, we all know how cute they look swimming in a mug of hot coco; however, this is not a healthy activity for your marshmallow and can result in significant weight loss and/or expiration.

  4. Don't Leave Unattended

    Do not leave your marshmallow in reach of small children or pets, they may mistake your marshmallow for an actual food item.

If these care instructions are carefully followed, you and your marshmallow have the potential to spend a long and happy life by each other's side
